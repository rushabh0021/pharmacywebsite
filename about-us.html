Clinic with Innovative Approach to Treatment

Welcome to Acorn Pharmacy. We are a local community pharmacy chain that provides a wide range of pharmacy services to patients across the Greater Manchester area.

I founded Acorn Pharmacy in 2009 with the aim of bringing the best pharmacy services and healthcare advice to the communities we serve. We now have branches in Whalley Range, Chorlton, Grove Village and Darwen. As well as an optician at our Grove Village Branch.

We are proud to be an independent pharmacy group employing local people and ensuring that we represent the communities we serve. Our aim continues to be to climb to ever greater heights of excellence, exceed our customers’ expectations, and push pharmacy into new frontiers in healthcare.

We would love to hear your feedback so please let us know by contacting our team.

Randon Pexon, Head of Clinic
